Asset-Copier For Kiwi Browser!

Step 1. Open Kiwi Browser app. Step 2. Go to "chrome://extensions", make sure developer mode is turned on, then click on the "+ (from .zip/.crx/user.js)" button, select the the zip file.
  • Step 3. Click the three dots on the top right of your screen, scroll down, click on the extension, and then enter the link of the asset/texture you want to copy. wait a few seconds (2-3 secs) and then Voila! the file will be downloaded!
